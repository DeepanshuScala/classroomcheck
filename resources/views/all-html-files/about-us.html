Our Story
Who We are

Classroom Copy is guided by four principles: customer service, passion for creation, commitment to excellence, and educator inspiration. Founded in 2022, Classroom Copy strives to provide a platform for educators to distribute electronic resources whilst offering the highest percentage on sales compared with similar platforms. This translates to educators supporting educators.

OUR MISSION

To provide a platform for educators to buy and sell original educational resources with greater profits going to the creators.

OUR VISION

To be the number one global destination for discovering great value and unique educational resources.

FOUNDING STORY

Classroom Copy was founded by Kristen Timoteo, a public school teacher who experienced first hand the hours of work that would go into creating resources for use both in the classroom and wider school community.

After putting her resources on other marketplaces to make some additional income, she was shocked to see just how little she actually received from the sale of her products after website commissions were taken out.

This inspired her to create Classroom Copy, a place where any educator can find essential resources, collaboration, and inspiration whilst supporting their fellow educators.
